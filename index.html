Ausbildung

A personal, offline-friendly guide

What is Ausbildung?

Ausbildung is Germany’s dual vocational training system that combines paid practical work with formal classroom education.

Who is it for?

Ausbildung is suitable for individuals who want practical skills, early work experience, and a pathway to long-term employment in Germany.

Basic Requirements

  • Completed secondary education
  • German language level (A2–B1, depending on field)
  • Training contract from a German employer
  • Valid passport and visa

Application Process

  1. Learn German
  2. Prepare CV and certificates
  3. Apply to Ausbildung programs
  4. Sign a training contract
  5. Apply for a German visa

Popular Ausbildung Fields

Common Ausbildung fields include Nursing, IT, Mechatronics, Hospitality, and other technical trades.

Salary & Reality

Trainees receive a monthly allowance that increases each year. While this helps with living costs, budgeting and financial planning are essential.
